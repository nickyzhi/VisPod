Thoughts on paper

22 April 2016

As a graduate student, my first priority is definitely publishing papers. So every time I finished a simple or complicated project, I tend to think what kind of improvements do I need to make, just for meeting the requirement of a conference paper.
Perhaps I shouldn't be anxious, it's my first semester for god's sake. As a computer science graduate student without pure CS undergraduate background, I need to worry about my qualify classes!
Graduate student, however, is a notorious hardworking job in the world. So I have to take care all stuff I'm facing now.
Oh, besides, I need to do more writing practices.
